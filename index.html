Bubu & Dudu

Bubu (the excitable panda) and Dudu (the gentle bear) — 布布 & 嘟嘟 — were created by Chinese artist Huang Xiao B in June 2018. Born as Weibo stickers, their sweet everyday love resonated first across China before hugging the world.

Meet Our Favorite Duo

Bubu (the panda)

Bubu is the playful spark—dramatic entrances, surprise hugs, and the loudest cheer when you need it. She thrives on cute chaos, turning every outing into a mini adventure. Underneath the mischief is a heart that loves big and tells you loudly.

Dudu (the bear)

Dudu is the calm anchor—soft, steady, and endlessly caring. He remembers your favorite snack, holds the umbrella, and beams whenever Bubu shines. His gentle presence makes naps sweeter, walks slower, and every day feel safe and loved.

Bubududu together is the love story: born as a June 2018 sticker pack on Weibo, now cherished by fans everywhere—plushies, lamps, hoodies, and smiles to match.
Chinese roots: 布布 (Bùbù, “little cloth/bear cub”) and 嘟嘟 (Dūdū, “cute pout”) began as Weibo stickers in 2018 and spread across China’s social feeds and WeChat chats as the go-to comfort couple set before going global.
